Research

My research spans multiple threads focused on optimization under uncertainty:

  • Developing novel frameworks for solving complex vehicle routing problems, with a focus on stochastic and robust optimization models that ensure stability across dynamic, uncertain environments.
  • Investigating adaptive public transport network redesign using multi-objective optimization techniques, with an emphasis on balancing service efficiency, operational costs, and dynamic adaptation to real-time uncertainties in passenger demand and travel times.
  • Integrating advanced machine learning techniques, including reinforcement learning and contrastive learning, to enhance optimization heuristics, especially in large-scale, real-time decision-making systems.
  • Exploring the application of decomposition methods, such as column generation and Dantzig-Wolfe, for solving large-scale stochastic vehicle routing and transport network problems, combining algorithmic rigor with practical applications.
